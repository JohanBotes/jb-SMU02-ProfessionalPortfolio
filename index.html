Johan acquired the Udexx Technology Group (Now Headquartered in Texas, USA) in June 2013 and created the "Udexx Business Cloud" with ERP and Accounting Software, Tax Automation, E-Commerce, Service Management, Route Management, Equipment Management, Customer Relations (CRM), Human Resources, Payroll, App API connectivity, and Payment Services.

A chartered accountant by qualification, Johan started his career more than 30 years ago with Deloitte.

Completing his Audit articles at Deloitte end 1992, between 1993 and 2007 he was Senior Partner at Burnan Chartered Accountants & Auditors. He returned to Deloitte for a five-year stint as CEO of Deloitte Mining Shared Services in 2007, during which time he learned a great deal about remote technology delivery, technology integration, API development, and Project management across multiple countries and time zones in the Technology and ERP global market.

He left Deloitte in 2012 to establish J.B. Udexx Capital as Holding Company to acquire the Udexx Technology Group.

Working with his leadership team Johan sets the strategy for the company’s continued expansion into the USA, Canada, UK & Ireland, Europe and Africa. Steering product & technology development, alliances, and overarching operations also underpins Johan's role as CEO & CTO.

Johan’s philosophy is that (1) thorough analysis, (2) full customer participation and (3) continuous non-stop user learning engagement are imperative to deploy the best possible solution for each unique business.

He favors cloud-based solutions and API integrations for a fully integrated experience. Johan seeks to engage customers for life with software products that deliver on what they promise.

As CEO of Deloitte Mining Shared Services, Johan gained valuable experience in remote technology delivery, technology integration, and project management across several countries in the technology and ERP global market.

In his free time, Johan enjoys running, enduro-motorcycle riding, and traveling the world with his wife Louise!
